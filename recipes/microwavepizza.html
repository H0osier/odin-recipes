Protein Shake

Description

Did you order pizza but not eat it all last night? You can heat it up and it still tastes good!

On this page I've included the ingredients and simple steps to microwave pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza that is at least 24 hours old.
  • A microwave

Steps

  1. Place the pizza on a microwave safe plate.
  2. Heat in the microwave for 15-30 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
