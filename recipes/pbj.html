Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a staple of American food. As simple as it is classic, the PB&J is one of the first dishes most people learn to make.

On this page I've included the ingredients and simple steps needed to make this meal.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter (Choose your preferred consistency)
  • Jelly
