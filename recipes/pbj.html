Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a staple of American food. As simple as it is classic, the PB&J is one of the first dishes most people learn to make.

On this page I've included the ingredients and simple steps needed to make this meal.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter (Choose your preferred consistency)
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Lay the two slices of bread onto a plate or similar flat surface.
  2. Open the peanut butter. With a knife, spread the peanut butter over one slice of bread.
  3. Open the jelly and spread it on the other slice of bread.
  4. Place the slices together.
  5. Enjoy!
